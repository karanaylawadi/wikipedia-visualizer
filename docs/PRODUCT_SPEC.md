# Wiki Visualizer

## Vision

Wiki Visualizer is an AI-powered visual encyclopedia.

Instead of reading long Wikipedia pages, users explore knowledge visually through elegant timelines and AI-generated summaries based strictly on Wikipedia content.

The experience should feel like Apple's website meets Linear meets Notion.

Minimal.

Elegant.

Fast.

Focused.

---

## Core Principles

- Wikipedia is the only knowledge source.
- AI never invents facts.
- AI only restructures and summarizes.
- Black background.
- Extremely clean interface.
- Every interaction should feel effortless.

---

## Homepage

Center aligned.

Contains only:

- Wiki Visualizer logo
- Search bar

- Black background

No extra text.

## Results Page

Contains:

- Hero image

- Timeline
- Related Articles
- Read on Wikipedia

---

## Timeline

The timeline is the primary storytelling component.

Instead of paragraphs, history is shown as elegant cards positioned along a vertical flowing timeline.

Every important event becomes an expandable card.

Example:

27 BC

↓

Julius Caesar Assassinated

↓

44 BC

↓

Augustus becomes Emperor

↓

14 AD

↓

Pax Romana
